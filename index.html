ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Olivia Molella. I am a BA candidate in the English and Professional Writing program at York University.

Since childhood, I have always been an avid fan of literature with a strong affinity for words. From a young age, I have always understood the value in telling a good story. I believe that so much of the power that we hold as individuals lies in our innate ability to communicate our ideas. As a passionate writer in our ever-changing digital landscape, I believe it is most important to create content that is unique and creative, while simultaneously inspiring change in others. I most value writing that creates the opportunity for others to share their own ideas and tell their own stories. I am especially interested in exploring how my role as a professional writer changes as the world does and in crafting a unique voice within such a diverse landscape.

With extensive skills in communication, research, and a strong attention to detail, I excel in content writing, as well as structural editing and copy-editing. Alongside this, I am able to examine the rhetoric of press and social media, and am especially skilled in applying these analyses to my own content creation. I believe in creating social media content that promotes both community engagement and fresh perspectives. I have also dabbled in creative fiction, graphic design, and blog writing. There are so many different ways to tell stories, and I especially believe in the power of multimodal elements to enhance this process. I am always seeking new opportunities to contribute my skills, knowledge and enthusiasm towards writing, communication, and all other forms of digital authorship.

"A writer's life and work are not a gift to mankind; they are its necessity." - Toni Morrison
